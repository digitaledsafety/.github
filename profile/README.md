## Hi there! 👋

Welcome to the official digital hub of the Digital Education & Safety Foundation (DESF). We’re a nonprofit on a mission to bridge the gap for underserved communities by making modern STEM/STEAM education both accessible and incredibly exciting.

### 🚀 What We Do
We believe the best way to learn is by doing. Our work sits at the intersection of high-tech engineering and proactive digital citizenship. From the classroom to the cloud, we’re helping the next generation build a better (and safer) digital future.

### 🛠️ What We’re Building
Explore our portfolio to see how we integrate hands-on innovation with real-world utility:

* Open-Source Utilities: Building tools that empower developers and educators alike.

* Web3 Innovation: Exploring the decentralized frontier and the future of the internet.

* Digital Citizenship: Ensuring "cool things" are built on a foundation of safety and ethics. Fostering a collaborative and secure environment through Minecraft servers and realms to prioritize a positive online experience and technical leadership.
